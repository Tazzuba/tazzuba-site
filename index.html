The Framework

Two Layers, One System

Algorithmic Stoicism

Classical Stoic thinking — Epictetus, Marcus Aurelius, Seneca — rebuilt as explicit if/then logic for the mind under pressure.

Every incoming event runs through one filter: is it within my control? True, and it gets your attention and effort. False, and it gets released before it costs you anything. No event gets processed twice.

Human Operations Engineering

If Algorithmic Stoicism governs the mind, HOE governs what's around it — the workflows, the teams, the systems a leader is responsible for.

It treats routine execution as something to design once and automate, often with AI agents handling the procedural work, so your attention stays on decisions only you can make.
